Before you begin using our service here is some important things to note

1 After you register your account, if you wish to become a creator you must apply to become one by clicking the Become a Creator button on the main page. 2 After you register your account, if you wish to purchase services/subscriptions you must create a wallet in your Settings page. After a while you will be able to see the account that is created. By default everybody starts with 1000 Algorand in our private network
